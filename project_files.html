Developing Humanitarian Data Standards:
An introduction and plan for 2014

Beginning in 2014, the United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs (OCHA) will lead a community standards effort to define a Humanitarian Exchange Language (HXL). HXL will help the humanitarian community capture and share high-priority response data across a network of disparate actors, such as agencies, NGOs, and governments. This strategy, writen by David Megginson, outlines DAP's contribution to the creation of HXL.
